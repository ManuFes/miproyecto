Introducción

Welcome to the tribute to a boxing legend, Mike Tyson. Nicknamed "Iron Mike" and known for his brute strength, aggressive style, and determination in the ring, Tyson rose from a tumultuous past to become one of the most recognized names in world boxing. Born on June 30, 1966, in Brooklyn, New York, Mike Tyson quickly stood out in the boxing world for his power and speed, becoming the youngest heavyweight world champion in history at the age of 20.
